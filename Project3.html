Xyrraiss Dominion
RogueLite Text-Based Adventure Game
A solo project with an 800+ line rogue-lite adventure game with a storyline, multiple character classes, turn-based combat, intricate damage calculation, differing rarity items, unique mobs, and bosses
User Input
User input is taken in the form of simple text of a number or yes/no and used to create a corresponding action in the game
Unique RPG Classes
Four classes are given as options to the user at the beginning of the game, each having their own unqiue stats, encouraging differing playstyles
Player Combat
Combat is turn based, with hostile mobs doing either physical or magic attacks
Storing of Values
Each class has 10 dynamic stats which change throughout the game from items and leveling, as well as 2 moves that have damage scaling with the character's current physical or magic attack stats
Items and Enemies
The game has a grand total of 40 items, each unique with it's own name, stat, stat amount, and description.
There are also 13 mobs, 6 bosses, 7 floors, and a final boss, Xyrraiss, each with it's own unqiue stats, power scaling throughout the game
Intricate Damage Calculation
Damage dealt and taken is calculated based off the player's move, current stats, enemy's move, and enemy's stats
